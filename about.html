In 2018, I left New York for Tel Aviv. Stopped freelancing as a content marketer, and started on path to hi-tech copywriting.

After writing for Israel's premier cybersecurity VC, YL Ventures, and the top technology PR firm, Headline Media, I finally landed at a magical branding workshop called Atreo.

Atreo is the world leader in B2B technology branding. I first learned the ropes as Branding Copywriter from some of the best in the business. Then, in early 2021, I was passed the torch as the Head of Brand Messaging.

Experience

Atreo, Head of Brand Messaging (Feb 2020-Present)

Within one year was promoted to full team lead. Today, own all Atreo messaging, and manage a growing department.

  • Writing core web and strategic copy for leading B2B tech brands—distilling sophisticated concepts and strategic messaging into crisp, concise copy that captures brand personality and tone of voice.
  • Developing brand narratives and stories that bring strategic directions to life and articulate the brand’s core positioning in just a few perfectly polished paragraphs.
  • Working closely with c-level executives on perfecting every aspect of their brand messaging—from technology and platform descriptions all the way to hero-level brand taglines.
